The Heart of Our Kitchen

Crafted with Passion,
Served with Pride

At Halime' Sultan, every dish honours the rich culinary heritage of Turkey. Our chef brings decades of authentic Turkish cooking tradition to Mauritius — from slow-grilled kebabs over open flame to hand-folded pides straight from the stone oven.

We source the finest spices, follow recipes passed down through generations, and serve each plate with the warmth and hospitality that Turkish culture is known for.

Two locations across Mauritius — one on the coast, one in the heart of Phoenix — the same soul in every bite.
